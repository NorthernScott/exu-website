# Watch Captain Doria Venn

## Role

A city guard watch captain in [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md) who arrived to disperse the mob that attacked the Moonlit Marvels & Menagerie carnival.

## Appearance

A woman carrying the visible weight of too many days without rest. The post-tsunami crisis has taken its toll.

## Personality

Exhausted and overworked, Doria Venn is trying to hold the city together with a guard force stretched far too thin. She is pragmatic and efficient -- she dispersed the mob at the carnival, took statements, and moved on. When the party helped with the situation, she thanked them and mentioned that the guard is stretched thin, adding that there have been “strange things in the city lately.”

## Connections

- Watch Captain of the Zhelezo in [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md)
- Responded to the mob attack on the carnival
- Grateful to the party for their help
