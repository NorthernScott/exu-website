# Brun the Behemoth MacDonald

## Role

A gentle half-orc strongman with the Moonlit Marvels & Menagerie carnival in [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md). [Helga Hirscka](../the-party/helga-hirscka.md) is interested in him.

## Appearance

A large and imposing half-orc, Brun cuts an impressive figure as the carnival’s strongman. Despite his size and the title “the Behemoth,” there is a gentleness to him that is immediately apparent.

## Personality

Brun is kind-hearted and community-minded. After the tsunami drove refugees from the surrounding countryside into Nicodranas, the carnival became a gathering place for displaced people, and Brun threw himself into the work of taking them in. He looked overwhelmed during the mob attack on the carnival, trying to protect refugees while the situation spiraled. He is doing his best under difficult circumstances.
