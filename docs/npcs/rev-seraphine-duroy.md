# Reverend Seraphine Duroy

## Role

Chaplain of the Marquis Demesne, the palace of the Marquess of [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md). She provided [Shin Cherou](../the-party/shin-cherou.md) with potions after Shin questioned her about events at the palace.

## Appearance

A human woman in her late 40s with dark hair pulled tightly back, salt showing at the temples. She has a sharp, narrow face and is always immaculately dressed in black and silver vestments.

## Personality

Composed and precise, Reverend Duroy carries herself with the careful discipline of someone who serves in a politically sensitive role. As Chaplain of the Palace, she must balance spiritual duties with the realities of court life. When Shin interrogated her, she provided useful resources -- two healing potions and a potion of truesight -- suggesting either genuine helpfulness or a calculated investment in the party’s success.

## Connections

- Chaplain of the Marquis Demesne (the Palace) in [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md)
- Serves under [the Marquess](the-marquess.md)
- Provided potions to [Shin Cherou](../the-party/shin-cherou.md)
