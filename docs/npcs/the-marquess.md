# The Marquess

## Role

The Marquess of Nicodranas and mother of [Lucien Sugea](../the-party/lucien-sugea.md). She is the ruler of the city and resides in the Marquis Demesne.

## Appearance

A woman of careful composure and aristocratic bearing, the Marquess carries herself with the practiced ease of someone accustomed to power. She is always impeccably presented.

## Personality

Aloof and meticulously composed, the Marquess projects an air of being unbothered by even the most extraordinary events -- including a tsunami that nearly destroyed her city. She is protective of Lucien to the point of aggression, and is not above using threats and bribes to ensure his safety. When [Shin Cherou](../the-party/shin-cherou.md) was present during an incident that put Lucien in danger, the Marquess bribed Shin with 500gp and obliquely threatened her order and her family. She challenges Lucien intellectually, once telling him that “philosophers never changed the world” when he complained about the influence of the temples.

> ## Motivation
>
> The Marquess is a secret follower and warlock of U’kotoa. She wants U’kotoa to gain power and influence in the region. She has been watching Caliban’s smuggling operation through Mara, a former Zhelezo she placed as a guard at Caliban’s warehouse.
>
> Her support for stopping Caliban is entirely self-serving:
>
> - Caliban’s usefulness has ended -- he weakened the city’s defenses, which allowed U’kotoa to “save” the city and gain credit and worship.
> - Caliban is now a loose end whose continued presence risks discovery of the larger plot.
> - Removing him preserves U’kotoa’s credit for saving Nicodranas.
> - The party becomes useful tools she can manipulate for future purposes.
>
