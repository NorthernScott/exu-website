# Reverend Haim Cortho

## Role

The leader of the Temple of the Matron of Ravens in the Skew district of [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md). He facilitated [Alarise Stormshore](../the-party/alarise-stormshore.md)‘s communion ritual.

## Appearance

An erudite man with the scholarly bearing of a lifelong clergyman.

## Personality

Kind and learned, Reverend Cortho carries himself with quiet authority. He leads the temple with a gentle hand and was willing to facilitate Alarise’s communion ritual, suggesting an openness to the extraordinary and a trust in the divine workings of his goddess.

## Connections

- Leader of the Temple of the Matron of Ravens in the Skew, [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md)
- Colleague of [Sister Thana](sister-thana.md)
- Facilitated [Alarise Stormshore](../the-party/alarise-stormshore.md)’s communion ritual
