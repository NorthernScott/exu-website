# Sister Thana

## Role

An elderly monk at the Temple of the Matron of Ravens in the Skew district of [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md).

## Appearance

An elderly woman with only one eye, Sister Thana has the bearing of someone who has seen much and suffered for it, but remains unbowed.

## Personality

Sharp-witted, skeptical, and kind beneath the gruffness. Sister Thana does not suffer fools, but there is a genuine warmth under her prickly exterior. She serves faithfully at the temple alongside [Reverend Haim Cortho](rev-haim-cortho.md).

## Connections

- Monk at the Temple of the Matron of Ravens in the Skew, [Nicodranas](../world/Locations/Nicodranas/index.md)
- Colleague of [Reverend Haim Cortho](rev-haim-cortho.md)
- Present when [Alarise Stormshore](../the-party/alarise-stormshore.md) underwent her communion ritual
