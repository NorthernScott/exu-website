## Session Log

### 845-03-15

- The party goes to the riot at the carnival. They don’t outright help the carnies or look for the vampires. The carnies are in the process of fleeing. The party starts helping to put out fires.
- Const. Dave Davidson of the Zelezho arrives; seems sweet and well meaning. Tried to arrest Helga but was dissuaded by her, Shin, and Lucien.
- Lucien hears people thanking “the sea itself” for saving the city from the tsunami after concluding that the “gods are missing.” He got a flash of U’kotoa’s eye and a sense of “anticipation, like someone sitting down to a big meal.”
- The carnival escapes, the mob dissipates, and the fire is extinguished.
- The party returns to their rooms at the palace. Alarise prays to her god, burning a piece of the finger she took from the Kuo-toa she killed. She feels a sense of peace.
- Lucien goes and tells his mother about his day, about the Kuo-toa, about Skrang, and then complains about the churches having too much power. His mother challenges him to do something about that, saying “philosophers never changed the world.” **He does not tell her about Dazz’s quest to take down Caliban.**

### 845-03-16

- Ada awakes and casts Alarm around her room.
- Shin awakes and prays.
- Helga does squats with Sushi the Dog, and tries coffee for the first time.
- We establish that there are coffee plantations nearby in the jungles around Nicodranas.
- The climate of Nicodranas is sub-tropical, like Savannah, Georgia or New Orleans. It was colonized by sailors from Marquette (North African analog) about 500 years ago, and they displaced the local Kinau tribespeople.

### Sluiceweave Place of Power (Dazz’s Quest)

- Party’s Marching Order: Alarise (Stealth roll: 16), Helga, Lucien, Ada, Shin.
- The party finds Marker XXIII and follows the path to Marker VII. They recognize the markers as ancient Marquesan numerals.
- They make it to the cavern and find the Ley Stone. The team collaborates to figure out that the censers are part of a magical ritual. The Ley Stone is using the energy of the Ley Line like a waterwheel to power the Sluiceweave. It is the engine at the heart of the machine. The censers are siphoning power like straws.
- Shin lights the Lantern and a fire elemental bursts forth like a fire tornado, and melts the censers into slag.
