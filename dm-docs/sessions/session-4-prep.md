# Session 4 Prep: After the Wave

## 1. Review the Characters

**Ada Glowsword**

- Interested in the theory that “The Sea Itself” rose to defend the city; old myths about sea-gods. Probably wants to report the impromptu shrines to the Cobalt Soul.
- Also interested in whether Yussa Erenis was responsible for the magical shield from Tidepeak Tower. Could be invited in by Toger Greytoe.
- Jungle ruins uncovered south of Raska Lake in the Quoraska Jungle tie to her scholarly interests.
- Ada caught a tuna while fishing.
- Ada messaged her mentor Onde Orsal via Sending.

**Alarise Stormshore**

- Has been given a holy task by the Matron of Ravens. Something went wrong with the ritual of Catatheosis. Her spiritual essence has been separated from her body since the ritual. She is essentially trapped in the Astral Plane.
- She needs someone to find her body and figure out what’s going on. She’s not sure if her body is a child or not - she made reference to time being weird.
- She suggested finding her old laboratory in the ruins of Avaliir (the Necropolis of Avaliir), somewhere in the Shattered Teeth. Materials and equipment there could help. She is having trouble accessing it in the Astral because of defences she put in place against interplanar threats. The location of her laboratory changes because of the spatial-temporal uncertainty of the Shattered Teeth.

**Helga Hirscka**

- Refugees from farthest flung farms and villages are entering the city. They are angry and scared: “If the City was spared, why weren’t we?” Substantial death-toll in the countryside.
- City-folk are suspicious of the outsiders. “Why weren’t they saved?” Some think “they were not chosen.”
- The circus has become a gathering place for refugees. Brun the Behemoth MacDonald and the carnival folk are doing their best to take in refugees.
- Helga has no regular income.
- Helga called Toger “Little Toe.”

**Lucien Sugea**

- Interested in treasure from wrecked ships. A merchant vessel from Marquette carrying spices and precious cargo may have been destroyed outside the harbour. Beachcombers are looking for the wreck.
- Lucien spoke in Shin’s mind for the first time.
- Lucien owns a sword that Ada’s parents made.
- Gets an allowance of 300gp/month, saves almost none of it, and is routinely just given stuff.

**Shin Cherou**

- One of the gods may have intervened to stop the wave. Leading contenders: Wildmother, Avandra, Kord.
- Offerings are being left at the Matron’s Lighthouse. The main temple of Avandra is full of worshippers and Shin, as a visibly marked priestess of Avandra, is accosted in the street for blessings.
- Gets 35gp/month (15 for chaplain role, 20 for work with Lucien), gives 30gp/month to her family, netting 5gp/month, with 2 mo. expenses saved.

**Unresolved from last session:**

- Nana Mori and all that nonsense happened.
- The party met Toger who obviously has a crush on Ada. They mercilessly teased him.
- Sushi caught a lot of fish.

**Character connections this session:**

- Ada/Shin: The gods vs. arcane theories about the Wave.
- Ada/Lucien: Lucien owns a sword Ada’s parents made.
- Helga: Refugee crisis and the carnival.
- Alarise: The Matron’s quest is a longer-term thread.

## 3. Potential Scenes

1. **The Sluiceweave Investigation** -- Capt. Marius in the Restless Wharf is looking for old entrances to the Sluiceweave, muttering about “things haven’t been working right down there for at least ten years.” His men are busy keeping order but he might offer to pay the party to investigate.
2. **Tidepeak Tower Crowds** -- A crowd gathers at a respectful distance around Tidepeak Tower, leaving flowers at its base. The party could push through and be invited in by Toger Greytoe, who wants their help getting people to leave so “Master Yussa doesn’t do anything rash when he returns.”
3. **Temple of Avandra** -- Offerings at the Matron’s Lighthouse, temple full of worshippers. Shin is accosted in the street for blessings.
4. **Sea God Shrines** -- Impromptu shrines to various “sea gods” are being set up around the docks and fisherfolk homes. Ada may want to report this to the Cobalt Soul.
5. **Refugee Crisis at the Carnival** -- Refugees are angry and scared. The carnival is a gathering place. Brun the Behemoth MacDonald is doing his best to take people in.
6. **Shipwreck Treasure Hunt** -- Merchant vessel from Marquette rumoured among the wrecks. Beachcombers are searching.

## 4. Secrets and Clues

1. The visible shield that extended from Tidepeak Tower was only part of its magical defences; it may have been Yussa Erenis who actually saved the city.
2. The Sluiceweave has not been “working right” for at least ten years according to Capt. Marius.
3. Something may have gone wrong with Alarise’s ritual of Catatheosis -- her spiritual essence has been separated from her body.
4. Alarise’s old laboratory in the Necropolis of Avaliir (Shattered Teeth) may hold materials to help, but its location shifts due to spatial-temporal uncertainty.
5. The low-lying jungle around the city was inundated and strange ruins have been uncovered south of Raska Lake, deep in the Quoraska Jungle.
6. A merchant vessel from Marquette carrying spices and precious cargo was likely among the ships destroyed outside the harbour.
7. Some city-folk suspect the refugees “were not chosen” -- tension is building between city and countryside.
8. The Kuo-toa attack may be tied to “The Sea Itself” theory popular among fisherfolk.
