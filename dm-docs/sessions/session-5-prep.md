## 5. Fantastic Locations

**Water Redirection Trap**

- See: Three wheels with clock face markings on a subterranean sewer crossing
- Hear/Smell: Pulsing water flow
- Feel: Mechanical, ancient infrastructure
- Feature: Each wheel controls the water direction, magnitude, and frequency respectively. They turned direction to 6 (down), magnitude to 1 (low), and frequency to 12 (slow). This will have an impact on the city’s water and sewer system -- it’s essentially an outflow valve.

- Hear/Smell: Humid and metallic air, rhythmic clangs like a heartbeat
- Feel: Oppressive industrial atmosphere, vibrations through the catwalks
- Feature: Single iron guillotine gate blocks the far exit, locked until the system is balanced

## 7. Monsters and Stat Blocks

### Balance Trap Mechanics

- **Four Balance Valves**: Massive wheels at the end of each catwalk. Turning one shifts the weight and flow inside the chamber (audible as groans of metal and hisses of steam).
- **Central Gate Mechanism**: Locked until the system is balanced.
- **Sump Pool**: 20ft deep; slippery rails along the sides. Anyone falling in must fight currents (Athletics DC 13 each round to avoid being pulled toward grates).

**Checks:**

- **Perception DC 13** -- recognize that the valves thrum at slightly different tempos.
- **Intelligence (Investigation) DC 14** or **Wisdom (Insight) DC 14** -- deduce that they must be turned in the correct order.

**Solution:** N, E, S, W -- each within one round of the last, to harmonize with the “heartbeat.”

**Failure:** If two wrong turns occur in a row, the chamber “coughs,” opening sluice vents that flood the sump -- water rises 5ft/round until reset (max 15ft).

### Trap: Pressure Gate Drop

If anyone crosses the central dais before the valves are solved, the pressure gate slams down.

- **Dex Save DC 15** -- avoid 3d10 slashing, otherwise trapped under the gate until others crank it up (Strength DC 16 as a group).
