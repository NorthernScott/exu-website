## 3. Potential Scenes

1. **Kuo-Toa Aftermath** -- Clearly the Kuo-Toa were worshipping U’kotoa. Unclear how much they had to do with the Wave. The huge chunk of amber is totally inert -- they just thought it looked cool. To the wrong buyer it’s useless and kinda cheap. To the right buyer (a jeweller or magician) it’s worth about 1000gp, depending on negotiation.
2. **Skrang the Revenant** -- The party encounters a pathetic creature, a Revenant, feeding on rodents. He tells them he agreed to be turned because he was sick and afraid of dying now that “the gods are no more.” Can potentially lead the group to a franchise of (mostly) pro-social Vampires.
3. **The Pantomime Pack** -- The Vampires Skrang is familiar with are the Pantomime Pack of clowns from the circus. They are very guarded but if clearly outed they are mostly happy to discuss their condition and indicate that they were turned in Port D’Amali.
4. **Quest from Dazz** -- Stop the Djinn Caliban at three locations: a warehouse in the Skew, a “Place of Power” in the Sluiceweave, and a tavern in the Restless Wharf. Get 3 boons.
5. **Mob Attacks the Carnival** -- A mob attacks the carnival. The tabaxi vamps are trying to escape.
